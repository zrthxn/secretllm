EXOCAD
The Candulor tooth lines PhysioSelect TCR and BonSelect TCR are included as digital tooth data in the exocad DentalCAD software.

The tooth data can also be found in the download area on the exocad website.
Candulor denture tooth libraries with open files supporting Monoblock Try-Ins and various production options including 3D-printed, milled and bridged teeth. Amann Girrbach "for AG" library set available.
